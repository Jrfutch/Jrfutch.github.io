PET SERVICES

Animal Shelter is committed to supporting you throughout the life of your pet

At Animal Shelter, we are committed to supporting you throughout the life of your pet with socialization and play group opportunities, training classes, pet boarding, a pet behavior helpline, and events for you and your pet.
Whether it’s a spa day, play date, sleepover, training class or veterinary care, we provide the best in pet services with highly trained, devoted associates. Services are conveniently located inside Animal Shelter office.

A WIDE RANGE OF AFFORDABLE PET CARE SERVICES

Our professional staff is glad to offer you high-quality pet care services including dog walking, pet sitting, boarding etc.

OUR SERVICES

Here’s just some of the pet services we offer

Pet Helpline

Our free Pet Helpline provides caring,
compassionate advice and resources for animal
issues you may be experiencing.

Pet Behavior and Training

Sometimes pet behavior problems seem
overwhelming, but many can be managed with the
application of knowledge and a little effort.

Boarding

Our Pet Boarding facility features overnight pet
boarding, doggy day care with indoor and outdoor
playing areas and a grooming spa.

Lost and Found Pets

Animal Shelter offers an online bulletin board to
help reunite lost pets with their families.

Pet Loss Support Group

When our companion animal dies, we feel a real
sense of loss and grief and to cope with it, we
recommend you to take part in our group.

Resources for Pet Owners

We understand that your pet is an important part of
your family and we want to help you locate
resources that will keep them healthy.
